## Rules

- A **seat** is a category of work the agency must get done. Not a job title.
- Each seat has **one owner**. Two owners = no owner. Unclear owner = no accountability.
- A person can own more than one seat. A seat cannot have more than one owner.
- **Each seat has a required profile shape.** The current holder is a ✅ **Fit**, a ⚠️ **Partial**, or a 🔴 **Mismatch** against that shape. Mismatches are structural findings, not personality comments. See `core/methodology/PERSON_PROFILE_STANDARD.md` § "Profile Fit (Operational)."
- When a seat changes hands, record the date here and in `EVENT_LOG`.
- "Vacant" is a valid answer. If a seat has no owner, say so — that is a finding.
