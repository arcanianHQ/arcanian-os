# SOP-07: Knowledge Sharing

> Team briefing, methodology updates, and institutional knowledge management.

---

## 1. Purpose

Ensure the entire team stays aligned on methodology evolution, client patterns, and operational decisions. Knowledge must flow systematically -- not through ad hoc messages. Every significant learning gets captured, every team member has access to current methodology.

## 2. Trigger

- Weekly standup cadence (every week)
- Monthly methodology review cadence
- Quarterly skills training cadence
- New pattern identified during client work
- Significant strategic decision made

## 3. RACI

| Activity | Founder | Research Lead | Delivery Lead | Analyst |
|----------|---------|---------------|---------------|---------|
| Weekly standup facilitation | A, R | R | R | R |
| Monthly methodology review | A, R | R | C | I |
| Quarterly skills training | A, R | R | R | I |
| CAPTAINS_LOG entries | A, R | C | C | I |
| KNOWN_PATTERNS updates | A | R | C | C |
| Skills documentation | A, R | C | I | I |

## 4. Systems & Tools

- CAPTAINS_LOG.md (decision log)
- KNOWN_PATTERNS.md (pattern library)
- Skills directory (`skills/`)
- Skills catalogue (`memory/skills-catalogue.md`)
- Team communication channel

## 5. Steps

### 5.1 Weekly Standup (30 min)

**Every week. No exceptions.**

| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| What's new | 10 min | New client insights, methodology observations, market signals |
| What's blocked | 10 min | Bottlenecks, resource constraints, decisions needed |
| What's next | 10 min | Priorities for the coming week, who owns what |

- Each team member prepares 2-3 bullet points before the standup
- Founder facilitates and captures action items
- Action items logged with owner and deadline

### 5.2 Monthly Methodology Review (60 min)

- Review all client engagements from the past month
- Identify: any new patterns? (behaviors, constraint types, cascade paths we haven't seen before)
- Ask: do any skills need updating based on what we've learned?
- Ask: does KNOWN_PATTERNS.md need new entries?
- Review: is the 7-Layer framework still capturing everything, or are there gaps?
- Output: documented review notes, action items for skill/pattern updates

### 5.3 Quarterly Skills Training (90 min)

- Demonstrate any new skills developed in the quarter
- Walk through updated skills (what changed, why)
- Hands-on: team members practice running key skills
- Review skills catalogue: any skills unused? Any gaps?
- Output: updated skills documentation, team confidence check

### 5.4 CAPTAINS_LOG Entries

- **Every significant decision gets logged.** No exceptions.
- Format: date, decision, rationale, who was involved, expected impact
- This is the institutional memory -- if someone asks "why did we decide X?", CAPTAINS_LOG answers
- Founder is primary author; team members can add entries with Founder review

### 5.5 KNOWN_PATTERNS Updates

- When a pattern is observed in 2+ client engagements, it becomes a known pattern
- Document: pattern name, description, how to detect it, which layers it affects, recommended response
- Research Lead maintains the file; Founder approves new entries
- Team has read access at all times

### 5.6 Skills Access

- All team members have access to the skills directory
- New skills are announced at the next weekly standup
- Skill usage questions addressed in standups or async

## 6. Escalation

- If weekly standup is missed 2+ weeks in a row --> Founder addresses immediately (this is a leading indicator of L1 breakdown)
- If a new pattern is identified but not documented within 1 week --> Research Lead escalates to Founder
- If team members report not understanding a methodology change --> Schedule ad hoc training session within the week

## 7. KPIs

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Weekly standup attendance | 100% (all team members) |
| Monthly review documented | Every month, no exceptions |
| Skills updated after new pattern | Within 1 week of pattern identification |
| CAPTAINS_LOG entries for decisions | 100% of significant decisions logged |
| Quarterly training completed | Every quarter |

## 8. Review Cadence

- **Weekly:** Standup happens
- **Monthly:** Methodology review happens
- **Quarterly:** Skills training happens + review this SOP itself
