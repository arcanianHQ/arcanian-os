# SOP-03: Proposal Delivery

> Proposal creation and delivery from diagnostic results to signed engagement.

---

## 1. Purpose

Convert diagnostic insights into a clear, compelling proposal that demonstrates we already understand the client's system. The proposal itself is proof of the methodology -- not a sales document but a diagnostic summary with a path forward.

## 2. Trigger

- Discovery call completed with 3+ constraint signals identified
- 7-Layer diagnostic first draft available
- Target Profile fit confirmed

## 3. RACI

| Activity | Founder | Research Lead | Delivery Lead | Analyst |
|----------|---------|---------------|---------------|---------|
| Proposal draft | A, R | C | I | I |
| Pricing calculation | A, R | I | I | I |
| Internal review | A, R | R | C | I |
| Send to client | A, R | I | I | I |
| Follow-up sequence | A | I | R | I |

## 4. Systems & Tools

- Diagnostic outputs (7-Layer, constraint map, [diagnostic profile])
- Proposal template (standard 3-tier structure)
- Offer ladder: Morsel / [Diagnostic Service] / Fractional CMO
- Email / direct communication channel

## 5. Steps

### 5.1 Draft from Diagnostic Results

- Open with: what we found (2-3 key diagnostic observations)
- Frame the problem in their language (use verbatim quotes from discovery call)
- Show the cascade: primary constraint --> downstream effects
- Do NOT include full diagnostic -- that is the deliverable they're buying
- Tone: honest practitioner, not sales pitch. Show you understand their system.

### 5.2 Three-Tier Offer

Structure every proposal with three clear options:

| Tier | Scope | Typical Range |
|------|-------|---------------|
| **Morsel** | Single diagnostic insight or quick audit (1-2 layers) | Free -- EUR 497 |
| **[Diagnostic Service]** | Full 7-Layer diagnostic + [Customer Need Framework] suite + walk-through report | EUR 2,000 -- 5,000 |
| **Fractional CMO** | Ongoing engagement: diagnose + stay to fix + change leadership | EUR 5,000 -- 10,000/month |

- Recommend the tier that matches the diagnostic findings
- Make clear what each tier includes and excludes
- [Diagnostic Service] always includes the walk-through (not just a document drop)

### 5.3 Pricing Calculation

- Base on: engagement complexity, company size, number of layers requiring intervention
- Factor in: team hours (Founder, Research Lead, Delivery Lead allocation)
- Document pricing rationale internally (not shared with client)

### 5.4 Internal Review

- Founder reviews full proposal
- Research Lead confirms diagnostic accuracy
- Delivery Lead confirms scope is deliverable within stated timeline
- All concerns resolved before sending

### 5.5 Send to Client

- Personalized email with proposal attached or embedded
- Subject line references their specific situation (not generic)
- Include: "This is based on our conversation and initial diagnostic. Happy to walk through it."
- Confirm next step: walk-through call or written questions

### 5.6 Follow-Up Sequence

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 0 | Proposal sent |
| Day 3 | Light check-in: "Any questions about what we sent?" |
| Day 7 | Value-add follow-up: share one relevant insight or pattern (not pressure) |
| Day 14 | Direct ask: "Would it help to walk through this together? If the timing isn't right, no problem -- the diagnostic stays valid." |
| Day 14+ | If no response, mark as dormant. No further chase. |

## 6. Escalation

- If internal review reveals diagnostic gaps --> Return to SOP-02 (schedule follow-up call)
- If client pushes back on pricing --> Founder handles directly; adjust scope (not price) if needed
- If proposal stalls beyond Day 14 --> Founder makes one direct call, then marks dormant
- If client wants a tier we believe is insufficient --> Flag honestly: "We can do this, but based on the diagnostic, this tier won't reach the root cause"

## 7. KPIs

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Proposal sent after diagnostic | Within 48 hours |
| Follow-up sequence executed | 100% on schedule |
| Proposal-to-contract conversion | Track monthly (no fixed target initially) |
| Internal review completed | Before every send (no exceptions) |

## 8. Review Cadence

- **Monthly:** Review all proposals sent -- conversion rate, common objections, pricing patterns
- **Quarterly:** Update proposal template based on what's working and what's not
