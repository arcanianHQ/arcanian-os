# 08 — Content Pipeline

> Generic SOP. Adapt per client in `clients/{slug}/processes/`.
> Owner: Fractional CMO | Tier: 3 | Review: Monthly

## Purpose
Turn content into a measurable revenue driver by connecting every piece of content to a conversion path, retrofitting top-performing articles with CTAs, and tracking content-sourced leads.

## Trigger
- New content published without a conversion path
- Monthly content performance review cycle
- Top article list refreshed (traffic-based)
- Content-sourced lead count drops below target

## Stakeholders (RACI)

| Role | R/A/C/I |
|---|---|
| Fractional CMO | A (strategy, CTA framework) |
| Marketing Director | R (execution, reporting, CTA placement) |
| Content Creator / Copywriter | R (content production, CTA integration) |
| SEO Agency | C (keyword strategy, content briefs) |
| Email / Automation Specialist | C (lead capture forms, sequences) |
| Sales Team Lead | I |

## Systems
- CMS / blog platform — content publishing, CTA placement
- Email marketing platform — lead capture forms, pop-ups, embedded forms
- Web analytics — content performance, conversion tracking
- CRM — content-sourced lead attribution
- SEO tool — keyword rankings, traffic data

## Steps

### Content-to-Conversion Flow
1. Every published content piece must have at least one conversion path:
   - **Primary CTA:** contextual to content topic (e.g., related guide download, consultation booking, product recommendation)
   - **Secondary CTA:** site-wide fallback (e.g., newsletter signup, general lead magnet)
2. Conversion path types by placement:
   - In-content CTA (inline text link or banner after key section)
   - End-of-article CTA (dedicated block)
   - Exit-intent or scroll-triggered popup (site-wide, frequency-capped)
   - Sidebar or sticky CTA (persistent on scroll)
3. Content Creator integrates primary CTA during content creation — not retrofitted after publication
4. Marketing Director verifies CTA presence before content goes live (part of publishing checklist)

### CTA Strategy by Content Type

| Content Type | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|---|---|---|
| Buying guide | Product recommendation / configurator | Email capture for personalized advice |
| How-to / tutorial | Related tool or service | Newsletter signup |
| Lifestyle / inspiration | Product collection page | Social follow / email signup |
| Technical / specification | Comparison tool / datasheet download | Consultation booking |
| Comparison / vs. article | Product page of recommended option | Email capture for decision support |

1. CTA type aligned to reader intent — buying guides get product CTAs, educational content gets nurture CTAs
2. CTA copy tested quarterly (A/B test top 5 articles)
3. No generic "Subscribe to our newsletter" without value proposition — always state what the reader gets

### Top Articles CTA Retrofit
1. Monthly: Marketing Director pulls top 20 articles by traffic from analytics
2. Audit each article for CTA presence and relevance:
   - Has CTA? Yes/No
   - CTA type matches content type strategy? Yes/No
   - CTA is functional (link works, form submits)? Yes/No
3. Articles missing or mismatched CTAs: Content Creator retrofits within 5 business days
4. Track retrofit completion in shared checklist — target: all top 20 articles have proper CTAs at all times

### Content Performance Review
1. Monthly review meeting (Fractional CMO + Marketing Director + Content Creator):
   - Top 10 articles by traffic — trend vs. prior month
   - Top 10 articles by conversions (form fills, clicks to product)
   - Content-sourced leads: total count, quality (link to `02-lead-lifecycle.md`)
   - Content utilization rate: % of published content that received >100 visits in last 30 days
2. Identify content gaps: high-traffic articles with zero conversions → prioritize CTA retrofit
3. Identify underperformers: articles with <50 visits after 90 days → decide: optimize, consolidate, or remove
4. Learnings documented and fed into next month's content brief priorities

## Escalation
- If content-sourced leads drop below target for 2 consecutive months → Fractional CMO initiates content strategy review
- If top 20 articles CTA audit finds >5 articles without proper CTAs → Marketing Director prioritizes retrofit sprint
- If content utilization drops below 70% → review content strategy alignment with SEO and audience intent

## KPIs

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Blog-to-email capture rate | 3-5% |
| Top 20 articles with active CTA | 20/20 |
| Content-sourced leads per month | 50+ |
| Content utilization (>100 visits/30 days) | > 90% |

## Review Cadence
Monthly — content performance review with metrics above. Quarterly strategy alignment with SEO Agency and Fractional CMO.
