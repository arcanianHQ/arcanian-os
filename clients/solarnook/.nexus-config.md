## Reporting currency

`EUR` — per `CLIENT_CONFIG.md`. Cross-currency aggregation rules per `core/methodology/CURRENCY_NORMALIZATION.md`:
- US entities (USD) and Dealers (USD) → convert to EUR via Databox normalized metric or ECB daily rate
- EU entity (EUR) → native, no conversion
- Block 2 footer must state the rate + date when cross-market totals are rendered
